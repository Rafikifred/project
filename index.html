Rwanda Trails – Site Plan

A planning document for my WDD 131 project

Site Name

Rwanda Trails – This name was chosen to represent eco-tourism and cultural travel in Rwanda. It emphasizes exploration of Rwanda’s landscapes, wildlife, and heritage.

Optional domain: rwandatrails.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide visitors with guides to Rwanda’s top destinations, eco-friendly travel tips, and resources for planning trips. It will include information about national parks, cultural highlights, accommodations, and an interactive contact form.

Scenarios

  • What is the best time of year to visit Volcanoes National Park to see gorillas?
  • How much should I budget for a week-long trip to Rwanda?
  • Where can I find eco-friendly lodges near Lake Kivu?

Color Schema

Selected colors and their usage:

  • Primary Green (#3ddc84) – used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Dark Navy (#0f1115) – used for backgrounds and footer.

Typography

  • Inter (sans-serif): Used for all headings (H1, H2, H3).
  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for body text and paragraphs.
